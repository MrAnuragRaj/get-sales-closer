Data Deletion Instructions

At GetSalesCloser, we respect your privacy and provide full control over your business data. If you wish to delete your account or any lead information stored within our Knowledge Vault, please follow the steps below:

1. Self-Service Deletion

Log in to your Command Center dashboard, navigate to **Engine Settings > Data Management**, and select "Wipe Lead Ledger" or "Delete Account." This process is instantaneous and irreversible.

2. Manual Request via Email

If you cannot access your dashboard, please send an email to anurag@getsalescloser.com with the subject line "DATA DELETION REQUEST."

Note: Requests must be sent from the registered email address associated with your account.

3. Processing Time

Once a manual request is received, our technical team will scrub all lead records, transcripts, and Knowledge Vault documents from our encrypted Supabase servers within 48 hours.
